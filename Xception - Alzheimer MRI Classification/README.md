Build the future of healthcare technology on your own machine. 🩺🧠

In this comprehensive Python tutorial, we are tackling a critical real-world problem: detecting Alzheimer’s disease from MRI scans using state-of-the-art Deep Learning. You will learn exactly how to build, train, and evaluate a robust Image Classifier using the Xception architecture and Transfer Learning.

Who is this for?
This guide is perfect for computer science students, AI engineers, and medical researchers looking to apply Computer Vision to diagnostic imaging.

<br/>

You’ll learn how to:

✅ Setting up your medical data pipeline in Python.

✅ The theory behind Xception and why it excels in medical imaging.

✅ How to implement Transfer Learning from pre-trained weights.  

✅ Training your Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) in Keras/TensorFlow.  

✅ Evaluating your model’s accuracy, loss, and confidence levels.
